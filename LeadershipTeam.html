Chase Zachary – President

Introducing Chase Zachary, a dynamic professional whose journey from NASCAR engine builder to President of Black Canyon Construction exemplifies his drive and versatility.

Chase’s career began in the heart of NASCAR country, North Carolina, where he honed his skills as a Toyota engine builder for the high-speed world of motorsports. His attention to detail and commitment to precision were evident in every engine he crafted.

In December of 2008, Chase embarked on a new chapter, moving to the Lone Star State of Texas. Here, he embarked on a career with Black Canyon Construction, starting as a Superintendent. In this role, he assumed responsibility for overseeing both commercial and residential projects, scattered across the vast expanse of Texas. His expertise ranged from ground-up construction to intricate remodels, showcasing his adaptability and unwavering commitment to quality.

Chase’s journey within Black Canyon Construction didn’t stop at the construction site. Recognizing his exceptional talent and leadership, the company elevated him to the role of Estimator. In the capacity, he undertook the intricate task of bidding on multi-million-dollar construction projects. His keen business acumen and knack for project assessment made him as invaluable asset in securing and executing large- scale projects.

In a remarkable turn of events, Chase Zachary was appointed as the President of Black Canyon Construction in 2022. This elevation to the helm of the company stands as a testament to his dedication, vision, and capacity to lead. Under his leadership, Black Canyon Construction continues to thrive and evolve in the ever-changing construction landscape.

Beyond his professional achievements, Chase finds balance in his personal life. He is a devoted husband and proud father of four daughters. His life is guided by his unwavering faith in God, with both family and faith occupying central roles. These values not only define his personal life but also permeate his leadership style, fostering a workplace culture grounded in integrity and compassion.

Chase Zachary’s journey from NASCAR to President is a remarkable tale of transformation and success. His passion for excellence, combined with his diverse skill set, continues to drive Black Canyon Construction to new heights, shaping the construction industry’s landscape in Texas and beyond.

Logan Green – Senior Project Manager

Born and raised in the heart of Texas, Logan Green brings a deep-rooted Texan work ethic and a wealth of experience to the realm of commercial construction. With an impressive 15-year tenure in the industry, Logan's journey began as a commercial superintendent, where he honed his skills on ground-up projects, ranging from dynamic retail spaces to essential medical facilities and convenient stores.

For four dynamic years, Logan served as a construction manager with one of Texas' most rapidly expanding convenience store brands. In this role, he spearheaded an ambitious program, overseeing the construction of an impressive 40 stores annually across the Lone Star State, while also leading the remodeling efforts of existing stores in Oklahoma and New Mexico.

In 2021, Logan joined the distinguished team at Black Canyon Construction, swiftly ascending to the position of Senior Project Manager. In this capacity, he orchestrates the day-to-day operations of the construction department, ensuring every project is executed with precision and excellence. Logan's impressive portfolio spans a wide range of projects, from contracts valued at $2 million to substantial undertakings exceeding $32 million.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Logan treasures his role as a devoted husband and proud father of four, ranging from seven years old to a precious newborn. His dedication to family is mirrored in his steadfast commitment to his faith, having undergone a meaningful conversion to Catholicism. This steadfast faith imbues both his personal and professional life, guiding his decisions with values of integrity, compassion, and unwavering dedication.

With Logan Green at the helm, you can be assured of a leader who not only brings a wealth of expertise to every project but also embraces a strong sense of family values and faith-driven ethics.

Wil Daly – Estimating

Wil Daley, an accomplished Estimator with a rich background in construction management. Raised in the picturesque town of Leakey, Texas, Wil's Texan heritage is ingrained in his work ethic and attention to detail.

Armed with a degree from Texas State University, Wil's journey in the construction industry began at Hard Rock Directional Drilling. Over six years, he honed his skills in surveying, project management, and estimating, establishing himself as a reliable and meticulous professional.

His tenure at Mastec Utilities, where he served as Project Manager and Estimator for three years, further solidified his reputation. Wil's ability to dissect complex utility projects and provide accurate estimations set him apart in the field.

Switching gears, he furthered his career as a Project Manager and Estimator at DEA Specialties, where he excelled in Division 10 subcontracting. His acute understanding of project intricacies and a commitment to precision ensured the successful execution of specialized construction projects.

Wil joined the Black Canyon Construction team in early 2021, where he swiftly progressed to become the Lead Estimator, a testament to his proficiency and dedication to excellence. His role has been instrumental in shaping the company's estimations and project success.

Beyond his professional pursuits, Wil finds solace in the company of his wife Ashley, daughter Clementine, and loyal companion Sophie. Together, they create a foundation of support that fuels Wil's passion for excellence.

Wil Daley - a meticulous Estimator with a dedication to accuracy and a commitment to delivering estimations of the highest quality.
